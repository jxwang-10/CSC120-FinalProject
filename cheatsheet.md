### Stats to change:  
Attack  
Defense  
Recovery  
Intelligence  
Speed

### Available attacks (enter the number):  
1:   Swing your sword  
2:   Cast a fire spell  
3:   Cast an ice spell  
4:   Cast a confusion spell  
5:   Cast a blinding spell  
6:   Retreat a few paces  
7:   Try to dodge  

^C to quit, or you will be prompted to quit or continue after beating all three bosses
